Ayamye.


It means Kindness in Twi, a language widely spoken in Ghana.

An act of kindness could be as simple as holding the door for someone or becoming part of a cause much bigger. Kindness inspires you to make a significant impact on the world for the better. A small act of kindness from us could mean a roof over the heads of dozens of underprivileged children. It could mean two square meals per day. It could mean providing basic education.

We at Ayamye are honored to be partnered with ThinkKindness.org to contribute towards the mission of helping an orphanage in Central Ghana.

We are seeking to raise $8,000 to build a school classroom. Your kindness would make a huge impact in those children's lives.
