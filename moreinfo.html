More Information


Our friends at ThinkKindness have been working hard to build a classroom for the orphanage children. Not only will this building provide these kids a space to safely learn in order to pursue their goals and dreams, they will even have working toilets, which is considered a luxury. When the Coronavirus pandemic hit, food for the kids and building materials got so expensive, all funds were depleted. Now the building sits waiting for the funds and construction to be completed.

To whom much is given, much is expected. We are blessed to live in an amazing community like Marlboro and we are grateful to be privileged. Every dollar you donate will make a big difference in completing our mission.
