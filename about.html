About


The orphanage we are supporting is VIMA children's orphanage, this orphanage is located in Volta Ghana, supports 53 African children. The VIMA Non-Profit Organization, which comprises the free donation of shoes and medical supplies, and the VIMA children Home supports children in need, with USA donors helping orphaned children. Since 2012, VIMA has grown to implement projects within highly deprived areas of Volta Region, Ghana. For more information visit the VIMA website: Click to go to VIMA's website
